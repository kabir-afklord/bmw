About BMW

BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke), founded in 1916 in Munich, Germany, is a global leader in premium automobiles and motorcycles. Known for performance, innovation, and luxury, BMW creates vehicles designed for drivers who demand excellence. The fascination of the BMW Group lies not just in our products and technologies but in our history too, which has been written by inventors, pioneers and engineers. We operate over 30 production sites around the world and a global sales network.
